Strawberry Cheesecake

Description

A strawberry cheesecake with strawberry and cheese and cake. Very good.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/4 cup of confectioner's sugar
  • 70 g. of butter
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 spoonfuls of baking powder
  • A bit of water for the dough
  • Filling of choice
  • 3 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of corn starch
  • 400 g. of strawberries
  • 1 cream cheese
  • 1/3 cups of sugar
  • 1 glass of whipped cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1/2 chocolate bar for melting
  • 3 spoonfuls of salt-less butter

Steps

  1. Put the dough ingredients together using a fork or somethin'. Pour some water on it to keep it consistent.
  2. Stretch out and pour on buttered pie tin. Insert in oven at like 500 degrees celsius for a while. Leave it cooling.
  3. While it cools, grab a pot and mix the sugar and cornstarch together, adding the fruit as well. Boil and mix for 2 minutes then let cool and pour over the dough.
  4. Separately, mix the cheese with the cup of sugar and vanilla. Pour that shit on the top of the fruit on the fruit.
  5. Melt the chocolate and add the butter. Let cool then apply over the cheese. Draw some funny stuff on it if you want then put it in the freezer.
  6. After 3 hours, pull it out and you can eat your terrible cheesecake! Awesome!
