Lucuma Mousse

Description

A mousse or bavarois made out of lucuma. I don't like lucuma at all but my mom loves this stuff.

Ingredients

  • 5 egg whites
  • 1 cup of sugar turned into syrup
  • 1 satchet of flavorless jelly
  • 300 g. of lucuma pulp (or 5 lucumas)
  • 4 spoonfuls of unwhipped cream

Steps

  1. Mix the egg white until it turns into snow. When they've become hard, add the syrup. Mix until cold.
  2. In a cup, put the flavorless jelly, add a spurt of cold water and mix. You will get a thick paste. Apply a bit of hot water and mix until fully disolved. Add to the egg whites.
  3. Mix the lucuma with the cream and add to the egg whites. Pour everything into a wet mold. Put in the fridge.
  4. After a few hours, your mousse is ready! Eat up and think about how you were expecting Mousse instead.
